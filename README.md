## About

This is the corporate website for Ktronics AB, an independent Swedish engineering and consulting company based in Katrineholm, Sweden. The company delivers engineering and consulting services across:

- Energy & battery solutions
- Power electronics & systems engineering
- Software, cloud & integration
- Embedded & hardware development
