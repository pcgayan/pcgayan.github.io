About Ktronics AB

Ktronics AB is an independent Swedish engineering and consulting company founded by Gayan Kandaudage (M.Sc. in Electronics, M.Sc. in Computer Science).

We combine experience from energy systems, power electronics, embedded development and cloud-native IT platforms. Our role is to help customers design solid architectures, solve hard technical problems and make existing systems easier to operate.

What we do

We help customers solve real problems in the field, in the workshop and in the data centre. Below are our main focus areas.

Energy & battery solutions

Design and consultancy for residential and small-business energy systems, including solar PV, hybrid and off-grid installations, battery storage and system optimisation for reliability, safety and efficiency.

Power electronics & systems engineering

Engineering support for inverters, chargers, power supplies and energy control systems. System-level design, integration, fault analysis and performance optimisation.

Software, cloud & integration

Architecture and development for integrations, APIs and data platforms. Experience with ERP, DevOps, observability and cloud-native environments for scalable, secure operations.

Embedded & hardware development

Consulting and development of embedded systems and electronics – from requirements and hardware design to firmware, testing and integration with existing IT infrastructure.

Operations & consulting

Beyond individual projects, Ktronics AB supports customers with ongoing IT and operations work. We can help you stabilise existing systems, plan new platforms or act as a technical partner when you need extra hands and brains.

IT consulting & operations

System design, network architecture, security reviews and IT operations support. We cover the full development life cycle – from concept and design to implementation, deployment and long-term operations.

Embedded & hardware consulting

Electronic hardware and embedded consulting from requirements and schematics to prototyping, testing and production preparation. Bridging electronics, firmware and backend systems into complete solutions.

IT & electronics products

Supply of selected IT and electronics products such as network equipment, inverters, UPS solutions and embedded hardware, including microcontroller-based devices assembled and delivered by Ktronics AB.
